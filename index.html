About Us

  • Welcome to the OWASP Sharda University Chapter!

    A vibrant community for tech enthusiasts across various fields such as software development, AI, web design, cybersecurity, and digital arts. We embrace all realms of technology, from ethical hacking to creative design, providing a platform for innovation and collaboration.

  • What We Offer:
    • Explore technologies like ethical hacking, AI, web development, and more.
    • Collaborate on multidisciplinary projects with like-minded peers.
    • Join specialized teams in areas such as design, social media, public relations, and technical development.
    • Participate in workshops, hackathons, and events hosted by tech experts.
    • Contribute to real-world projects, build your portfolio, and gain hands-on experience.
  • Our Goal:

    Our chapter is dedicated to fostering a collaborative environment where everyone can thrive, grow, and make a meaningful impact in the tech world.
